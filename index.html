My Travel Destination

Busan, Korea

Busan is my first destination if I ever travel to Korea! It is a beautiful and vibrant city near the seaside. The city enjoined with Southeastern zone including Ulsan, and South Gyeongsang is the largest industrial area of South Korea. Busan is known as the summer capital of South Korea with Haeundae beach as the largest beach in the country. Busan has the busiest ports in Korea. It ranks in 5th in busiest ports in the World.

What to do there?

Busan, a large port city in South Korea, is known for its beaches, mountains and temples. Busy Haeundae Beach features the Sea Life Aquarium, plus a Folk Square with traditional games such as tug-of-war, while Gwangalli Beach has many bars and views of modern Diamond Bridge. Beomeosa Temple, a Buddhist shrine founded in 678 A.D., is at the base of Geumjeong Mountain, which has hiking trails.

What to Eat in Busan?

Here are some must-try foods when you visit Busan:

  1. Dwaeji Gukbap (Pork Soup) Dwaeji Gukbap
  2. Bokguk (Pufferfish Soup)
  3. Ganjang Gejang (Soy Sauce Crab)
  4. Milmyeon (Chilled Wheat Noodles)
  5. Jagalchi Seafood
  6. Ssiat Hotteok (Seed-filled Pancake)
  7. Yukgaejang (Spicy Beef Soup)
  8. Samgyeopsal (Grilled Pork Belly)
